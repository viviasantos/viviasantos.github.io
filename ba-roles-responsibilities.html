A didactic project that explains the Business Analyst role through a visual sequence, practical scope, key responsibilities, and the skills that support clear analysis.

  • Use the final skills slide to close with the capabilities that support the role.

Frameworks and best practices

The deck is easier to explain when the role is tied to simple frameworks that help structure analysis, prioritization, and decision making.

Practice

Stakeholder mapping

Identify who is impacted, who decides, and who needs to stay informed so analysis does not happen in a vacuum.

Practice

RACI

Clarify roles and ownership so responsibility stays visible across delivery and collaboration.

Framework

SWOT and MoSCoW

Use SWOT to understand the context and MoSCoW to prioritize what matters most in the material or project.

Framework

BPMN and process mapping

Keep the flow visible so the audience can see how work moves from problem to outcome.

Practice

5 Whys

Use a simple root-cause question chain when a problem needs to be explained clearly.

Practice
